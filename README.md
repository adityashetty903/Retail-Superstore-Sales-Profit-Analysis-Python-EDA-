# 📊 Retail Superstore: Sales & Profitability Analysis

## 📋 Project Overview
An in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a retail superstore dataset to uncover actionable business insights. This project evaluates historical sales trends, diagnoses profitability patterns, and analyzes customer segments to support data-informed decision-making.

---

## 🔍 Key Areas Analyzed
- **Monthly Sales Trends:** Identify seasonal peaks and troughs to improve inventory planning and forecasting.  
- **Category & Sub-category Performance:** Distinguish between high-volume drivers and high-margin assets.  
- **Profitability Patterns:** Map product- and region-level margins to pinpoint revenue leakage.  
- **Customer Segment Analysis:** Compare Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments for sales and profit contribution.  
- **Sales-to-Profit Efficiency:** Analyze discounting strategies to understand where high sales volume does not translate into proportional profit.
